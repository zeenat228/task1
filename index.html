Freqently Asked Questions (Q)

We know you may have questions, and we're here to help. Below you'll find
answers to the most frequently asked questions about our services, booking
process, payment methods, and more. If you do not find the answer you're
looking for, feel free to reach out to our support team for further assistance.

I should I bring to a yoga class?

Bring a yoga mat, a water bottle, and a towel. comfortable clothing that allows
for a full range of motion is also recomended. Some Studios provide mats and
other props, but it's always a good idea to check in advance.

Do I need to have prior experience to join the class?

No prior experience is needed! Classes are designed for all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners.

What types of yoga do you offer?

Bring a yoga mat, water bottle, towel, and comfortable clothing.

How hourly should i arrive for the class?

It's recommended to arrive at least 10 minutes early to settle in and prepare for class.

What if I have a medical condition or injury?

If you have any medical conditions or injuies, please consult your instructor before class so they can provide modifications and ensure your safety
